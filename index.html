My Journey

I am a professional Interior Designer who has been involved in the industry for over 10+ years. My specialties are in the area of interior and exterior design, architecture, and arts. While designing I keep in mind that the design is sufficient enough to make them meaningful, unique and interactive.

  • Feb 2023

    Accenture Product Design Virtual Internship

    Completed Accenture Product Design Virtual Internship Experience Program with Forge.

  • Oct 2022 - Dec 2022

    Google UX Design Professional Certificate

    My main focus on this course was to learn about the interaction that users have with products, like websites and mobile apps in order to make these everyday interactions useful, enjoyable, and accessible.

  • 2019 - Present

    Freelancer

    I am a freelancer working on UX/brand design with a passion for creating meaningful and impactful user experiences. My strength lies in combining creative concepts with technical knowledge to develop user interfaces and products that are both visually pleasing and highly functional. I have a deep understanding of user-centered design principles, user research, usability testing, interface design, prototyping, and design systems.

  • Nov 2017 - Jul 2022

    Senior Architect - Alap Kamdar Architects

    After gaining experience as an Intern, I tried my fortune to apply for a job in an architecture company as a junior architect. I gained many experiences there, particularly in the area of interior, and exterior design, Residences, farmhouses, cafes, and corporate offices. Only around 1 year after my acceptance, In January 2019 I was promoted to the position of Assistant Architect of my team. In January 2021 I was again promoted by the management to become a Senior Architect.

  • Sep 2016 - Mar 2017

    Internship - Tor Arch Architect

    While continuing my post-graduate program at the university, I worked as an intern architect. My biggest contribution at that time was to a small company namely Tor-Arch Architecture firm.

  • 2011 - 2016

    Bachelors in Architecture

    In my school days I was good in designing and sketching. So I decided to make my interest a full-time career. After 5 years of stressfull education and a constant race between assignments, submissions, model creation and drafting, I earned my licence to practice architecture in India.
